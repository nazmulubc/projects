The Shift to Problem-First Empathy

Early in the term, the focus was purely on building an automated toxic gas emission monitor. However, analyzing the problem through the Iceberg Model revealed that fixing surface-level issues without understanding underlying systemic structures leads to failure.

Surface Problem: Building the Physical Automated Sensor

Underlying Systemic Context:

  • Environmental specialists' human fatigue

  • Complex human infrastructure environments

Insight: You must fall in love with the problem, not the product.
